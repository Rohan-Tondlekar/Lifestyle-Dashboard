Medical Note — Act Early

The pattern observed (temple recession + crown thinning at 24) is characteristic of androgenic alopecia. Ketoconazole + zinc + scalp massage are supportive. The two evidence-based medical treatments are Minoxidil (topical, OTC in Germany — "Regaine Männer" at dm/Rossmann) and Finasteride (oral, prescription only). See a Hautarzt to confirm diagnosis and guide treatment. Earlier = more hair preserved.
